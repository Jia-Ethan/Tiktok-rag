# Public feedback questions

Use these questions if you want to collect focused early feedback on `video-rag`.

## Workflow fit

1. What kind of video knowledge workflow are you trying to build?
2. Are downloaded local video files already your normal input boundary?

## Next step after transcription

3. After transcript generation, what do you want next: retrieval, summary, knowledge organization, or prompt context construction?
4. What is the first missing step that stops this repo from being useful to you?

## Input reality

5. How long are the videos you usually work with?
6. What language or language mix do they contain?
7. Are they usually clean speech, noisy recordings, multi-speaker content, or something else?
